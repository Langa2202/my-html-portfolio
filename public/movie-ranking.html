Mike Lang's Favorite Movies

In no particular order...

Silence of the Lambs

This is one of the best serial killer movies of all time. Anthony Hopkins and Jodie Foster's performance is out of this world!

Se7en

One of my favorite Brad Pitt movies which rivals Silence of the Lambs as one of the best serial killers movies of all time. Also stars fan favorite Morgan Freeman.

There Will Be Blood

Daniel Day Lewis is my favorite actor and his performance in this is truly captivating. Incredible flick!
